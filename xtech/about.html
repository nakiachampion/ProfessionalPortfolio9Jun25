About Xceptional Technologies

Empowering Detroit through Technology

Our Story

Xceptional Technologies is a Detroit-based Managed Service Provider (MSP) specializing in Ubiquiti UniFi's full-stack ecosystem. We deliver enterprise-grade solutions for networking, EV charging, VoIP, access control, and smart IoT tailored specifically for small businesses and entrepreneurs.

Founded with a commitment to bridging Detroit's digital divide, we combine cutting-edge technology with community-focused digital literacy programs, creating pathways to economic opportunity while providing scalable, affordable solutions.

Mission

To empower Detroit communities through accessible technology, fostering digital literacy and providing pathways to economic opportunity.

Vision

To be the leading provider of Ubiquiti solutions in Detroit, renowned for our community impact and commitment to closing the digital divide.

Values

  • Technical Excellence
  • Accessibility

Our Certifications

Our team maintains comprehensive Ubiquiti certifications to ensure expert-level service across all UniFi platforms:

UFSP

UniFi Full Stack Professional

Foundation course covering Global Site Manager, UniFi Network, Protect, Access, Connect, and Identity

URSCA

Advanced network infrastructure and cybersecurity expertise

UNP

UniFi Network Professional

Expert-level UniFi network management including Wi-Fi 7

UBWS

UBWA

Advanced WISP network administration

UPSA

Integrated UniFi Protect and Access systems expertise
